Power Lens XY aligner

Cube insert

Tools

  • 1 Soldering iron

Mechanical Components

  • 2 Heat insert
  • 2 M3 x 25 mm hex head screw

Optical Components

  • 1 Linear Beam Generator Lens

Printed Parts

  • 1 Power Lens XY aligner A-Adapter
  • 1 Power Lens XY aligner base

This component is designed to be used inside a UC2 cube, allowing for better laser beam positioning through XY control.

Assembly:

Step 1: Screws

  • Place 2 heat inserts into the XY aligner base using the Soldering iron

  • Screw the hex head screws

Step 2: Lens Adapter

Place the springs and screw the A-Adapter

Step 3: Lens

Place the Lens in the [A-Adapter].

Caution

Do not touch the lens directly to avoid getting it dirty or scratched.

Step 4: Put it in a cube!:

Take one of your UC2 cubes and place your assembled piece in it. You can purchase the cube or 3D print it, either option works fine.
