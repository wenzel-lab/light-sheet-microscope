Flex Holder for Cylindrical Lens

Cube insert

Tools

  • 1 2mm Ball-end Allen key

Mechanical Components

  • 4 M2.5 x 8 mm screw

Optical Components

  • 1 LJ1328L2-A Lens
  • 1 LJ1567L2-A Lens

Printed Parts

  • 1 Holder Lid
  • 1 Lens - Holder
  • 1 Taller Lens - Holder

There are different types of lenses, which is why this model integrates a flex mechanism that allows to have lenses with small width differences, and still be held.

Assembly:

Step 1: Screw the printed parts:

Take the flex desired piece, which can be the Lens - Holder or the Taller Lens - Holder and close it with the Holder Lid using 2mm Ball-end Allen key and 4 M2.5 x 8 mm screw

Step 2: Place the lens:

Take the LJ1328L2-A or the LJ1567L2-A lens and place it into the Lens - Holder or Taller Lens - Holder respectively.

Step 3: Put it in a cube!:

Take one of your UC2 cubes and place your assembled piece in it. You can purchase the cube or 3D print it, either option works fine.
