Camera-container

Tools

  • 1 2.5mm Ball-end Allen key

Electronic Components

  • 1 High-speed camera

Mechanical Components

  • 2 M3x15mm screw
  • 4 M3x25mm screw

Optical Components

  • 1 Camera Lens

Printed Parts

  • 1 Double Cube Lens Holder A
  • 1 Double Cube Lens Holder B

This part consists of a double cube adaptation designed to hold a specific type of lens. These parts are screwed together to maintain the position of the lens and allow for manual focus adjustments.

Assembly:

Step 1: Connect the camera:

Thake your [camera][[High speed camera]] and the lens and screw them together.

Step 2: Positionate the pieces:

  1. Place the mounted camera on top of the Double Cube Lens Holder A, matching the diameters of the [lens][Lens] with the ones in the printed part.

  2. Do the same thing with the Double Cube Lens Holder B, placing it on top of the other two.

Step 3: Screw:

Take the M3x15mm and the M3x25mm screws, place them into the plastic and tighten using a 2.5mm Ball-end Allen key, leaving the shorter screws in the holes furthest from the lens, as shown in the image.

Ensure that the camera is positioned correctly.
