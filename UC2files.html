UC2 Base Cubes

Tools

  • 1 2.5mm Ball-end Allen key
  • 1 3.5mm Ball-end Allen key

Mechanical Components

  • 4 M3x15mm screw
  • 4 M5x10mm inner screw

Printed Parts

  • 1 Base Cube
  • 1 Cube Top
  • 1 Puzzle Base
  • To assemble these printed cubes you will need 4 M3x15mm screw for the Cube Top and an M3 Allen key to insert them into the Base Cube.

  • To place one or many cubes into the Puzzle Base insert 4 M5x10mm inner screw using the M4 Allen key into the Base Cube, then connect both parts.
